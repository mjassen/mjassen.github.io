w̶i̶e̶l̶d̶s̶i̶l̶v̶e̶r̶.̶c̶o̶m̶ values vacationing with friends. Vacationing sizzles!
w̶i̶e̶l̶d̶s̶i̶l̶v̶e̶r̶.̶c̶o̶m̶ 非常重視跟朋友一起去旅行。去度假令人超級興奮的!

A week vacation with friends costs 6,973 silver-grams.† (sizzling meals included!)
跟朋友一個禮拜旅行要花錢花 6,973 銀克。† (包括食美味的餐食!)

If we spend hard-earned work income on food & housing, then how to pay for much-loved vacations?

If we use interest to pay for vacations, then we'll fancy ourselves very clever and officially thriving.

Financial literacy empowers this.

In summary, w̶i̶e̶l̶d̶s̶i̶l̶v̶e̶r̶.̶c̶o̶m̶ is about vacationing, loving, working, investing, and thriving.
