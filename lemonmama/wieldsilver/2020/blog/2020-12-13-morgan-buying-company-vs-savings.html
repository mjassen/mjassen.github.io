Buying a Company and Opening a Savings Account

In June I considered putting my money in a bank savings account that yields 1% per 18 months. 

But then I looked at the yearly dividend yield for buying company stock shares of that same bank and the yield was 6% per 12 months.

People's United Bank (NASDAQ:PBCT)

Ironic?
