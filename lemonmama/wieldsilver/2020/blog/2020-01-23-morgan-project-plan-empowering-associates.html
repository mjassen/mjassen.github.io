Project Plan for 2020 Weeks 04 and 05 - Through my Work Project Empowering my Associates


2020-01-23 by author Morgan Jassen on w̶i̶e̶l̶d̶s̶i̶l̶v̶e̶r̶.̶c̶o̶m̶

This is a plan for a two-week growth and learning project for weeks 04 & 05 of 2020.

In the "Financial Freedom" book¹ on money topics, the author persona Suze Orman advises to ultimately put people before money.


- In these two weeks I will learn about “Through my Work Project Empowering my Associates”. In other words at work adding value by helping my colleagues and clients.

- After the two-week project, I'll blog a “Final report” describing what happened during the project, and what I learned. (This report will be based on daily journal entries that I kept during the two-week project.)


¹ Financial freedom (Audiobook), Author: Suze Orman (Publisher: Carlsbad, CA : Hay House Audio, ℗2002.) http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/49827367
