Silver Gram Value Calculations Blog Post Page


This blog post calculates the current price of a silver-gram (one gram of silver in USD$).

Specifically calculated is how many silver-grams per 1 dollar.

This post may be updated with a newer price, perhaps quarterly.


~~~
2021-01-25 Update:
~
Silver Spot XAG | USD $25 per 1 oz. | https://www.reuters.com/quote/XAG=X
( 28g. / $25 ) = ( 1.12g per dollar)
~~~


~~~
2020-12-29 Silver gram calculation:
~
Silver Spot XAG | USD $26 | https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=XAG=
1 ounce = 28 gram, therefore:
( $x / 1g. ) = ( $26 / 28g. )
2020-12-29 now: SILVER 1 gram = USD $0.93 (= 1.08g per dollar)
~~~
