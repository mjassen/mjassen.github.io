Remix Paradigm for Life Project Sprints

—- pick a person
—- pick a project (e.g.theirs ir if not then yours)
—- work that project with that person for two weeks sprint
—- then blog the results report (obfuscated or not)

Thats it!

One “priority “ person & project per fortnight.
