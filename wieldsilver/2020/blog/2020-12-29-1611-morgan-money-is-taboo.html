Money is Taboo


The verdict is in.

Money is taboo to many people.


Evidence 1:

In this July 8, 2020 Secrets of Wealthy Women podcast episode the persona of Jane Schwartzberg - second half — about 15m.30s. in to the episode -- NAILS IT about money being taboo! 
Like: '...growing up in a household where money is taboo..." 
But says we need to blow that up and open the conversation.
