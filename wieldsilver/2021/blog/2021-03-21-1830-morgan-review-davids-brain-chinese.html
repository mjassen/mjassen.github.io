Review of “The Market Cap Game Show： Inside David’s Brain”


篇文章評論的播客單集是「市值益智節目：大衛的腦海中」*
This is a review of The Market Cap Game Show: Inside David's Brain* (podcast episode).

主持人大衛・加拿說大家來玩「市值益智節目」。
Host David Gardner says we will play "The Market Cap Game Show".

主持人大衛・加拿介紹 馬利亞・加拉格爾 和 艾蜜莉・弗利彭。
Host David Gardner introduces Maria Gallagher and Emily Flippen.

他們說一個公司的市場資本總額(市值)等於已發行股票乘以股票價值。
They say that a company's market capitalization is its total existing shares times its share price.

這也就是說那個公司全部發行的股票的總計價值。
This is effectively the total current price of all the company's shares.

主持人大衛・加拿說學習一個公司的市值會聰明的。
Host David Gardner says it can be wise to know a company's market cap. 

主持人大衛・加拿說 Pinterest 公司 (PINS) 的市值是~490億銀克†。
Host David Gardner says Pinterest company (PINS) is ~49 billion silver-grams† market cap.

為了開心他們聊天關於甘草糖。
They chat for fun, and talk about licorice.

他們一邊談話關於股票投資，一邊玩。
They play while talking about stock investing. 

他們說Boston Beer Company(SAM)萬里富曾經推薦過的一張股票。
They say The Boston Beer Company(SAM) has been a Motley Fool Stock Advisor pick.

他們說SAM市值現在是~120億銀克。
They say SAM market cap is now ~12 billion silver-grams.

主持人大衛・加拿說，所謂的公司，是社會文化的一組成部分。
Host David Gardner says that companies are part of people's culture.

我自己發現，這個播客單集討論的公司之間，許多公司的市值***在60億與560億銀克之間***。
My own note is that for many of the companies talked about on this episode, their market caps were ***between 6 billion and 56 billion silver-grams***.

我自己發現，谷歌公司的市值現在是~1兆銀克(二零零一年一月)。
My own note: Alphabet company market cap is now ~1 trillion silver-grams (in Januay 2021).

最後，這次是我第一次收聽到過萬里富所謂「市值益智節目」播客單集之一。於此我學會了很多關於市場資本總額(市值)。
In conclusion, this was my first listen to any of the Motley Fool's "Market Cap Game Show" episodes. From this I learned a lot about market capitalization.
